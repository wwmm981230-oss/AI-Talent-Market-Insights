# AI Talent Market Insights

First-hand observations on AI talent markets, technology trends, and career development, based on AI recruiting practice and 300+ conversations with professionals across the industry.

## Why This Project

During my work in AI recruiting, I have been in continuous contact with companies and professionals in areas such as:

* Large Language Models (LLMs)
* Autonomous Driving
* Embodied AI
* AI Infrastructure

While there is abundant discussion about AI technologies and products, systematic observations on talent movement, hiring trends, and organizational demand are relatively limited.

This project was created to:

* Document long-term observations of the AI talent market;
* Exchange insights with recruiters, HR professionals, and talent acquisition practitioners;
* Help technology professionals better understand industry trends and career opportunities;
* Record my own learning journey in AI recruiting and talent research.

I am an AI recruiting practitioner with experience in both in-house recruiting and executive search. My work focuses on AI, autonomous driving, embodied intelligence, and AI infrastructure talent markets.
